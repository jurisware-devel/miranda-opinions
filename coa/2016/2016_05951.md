{**28 NY3d at 940} OPINION OF THE COURT

On review of submissions pursuant to section 500.11 of the Rules of the Court of Appeals (22 NYCRR 500.11), order affirmed. Defendant's sole argument, concerning whether the trial court followed the proper procedure for permitting witnesses to testify anonymously, is unpreserved for our review.{**28 NY3d at 941}

Concur: Chief Judge DiFiore and Judges Pigott, Rivera, Abdus-Salaam, Stein, Fahey and Garcia.
