{**21 NY3d at 916} OPINION OF THE COURT

On review of submissions pursuant to section 500.11 of the Rules of the Court of Appeals (22 NYCRR 500.11), order modified by reducing defendant's conviction of depraved indifference murder in the second degree to manslaughter in the second degree and remitting to County Court, Orange County, for resentencing and, as so modified, affirmed ([3]see People v Bussey, 19 NY3d 231 [2012]).

Concur: Chief Judge Lippman and Judges Graffeo, Read, Smith, Pigott and Rivera.
