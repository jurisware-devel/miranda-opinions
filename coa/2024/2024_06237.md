{**42 NY3d at 1074} OPINION OF THE COURT

Memorandum.

The order of the Appellate Division should be affirmed.

The argument defendant raises on this appeal, whether the initial stop of defendant constituted a level four encounter with police, is unpreserved for our review (see CPL 470.05 [2]; [People v Graham, 25 NY3d 994, 996-997 [2015]](https://miranda.jurisware.com/case/2015_03767/); see generally People v De Bour, 40 NY2d 210 [1976]).{**42 NY3d at 1075}

Chief Judge Wilson and Judges Rivera, Garcia, Singas, Cannataro, Troutman and Halligan concur.

Order affirmed, in a memorandum.
